# 🎯 GitHub Profile Optimization Guide for Data Analysts

To make your profile look like a professional Data Analyst, please follow these simple steps on GitHub.

---

### 1. Update your Bio 📝
Go to your GitHub profile and click **"Edit profile"**. Copy and paste this bio:

> **Data Analyst | Python, SQL, Tableau | Identifying trends and solving complex problems through data-driven insights.**

---

### 2. Pin the Right Repositories 📌
On your profile page, click **"Customize your pins"**. Select these two in this order:
1. `Data-Analytics-Portfolio` (This is your main showcase)
2. `MIND-BEHIND-CODE-PORTFOLIO` (This shows your visualization skills)

*Remove any Java or unrelated projects from your pins to keep the focus on Data.*

---

### 3. Add Repository Topics (SEO) 🔍
Go to your [Data-Analytics-Portfolio](https://github.com/iamharsh00/Data-Analytics-Portfolio) repository. Click the **gear icon (⚙️)** next to "About" and add these tags:

`data-analytics`, `python`, `sql`, `machine-learning`, `exploratory-data-analysis`, `pandas`, `visualization`

---

### 4. Link your LinkedIn & Portfolio 🌐
In the **"Edit profile"** section, make sure to add:
- **Website:** Your live Portfolio link (once you host it)
- **Social accounts:** Link your LinkedIn profile URL.

---
**Why this matters?** Recruiters often spend less than 30 seconds on a profile. These changes ensure they immediately see your Data Analytics skills and your best work.
